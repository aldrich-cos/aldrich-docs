Cost model overview

Four lines of cost. One target ratio.

  • People — large in absolute dollars, small at PK cost base
  • Cloud infrastructure — scales super-linearly with user count
  • LLM API usage — scales with users × action volume (global pricing)
  • Software, legal, compliance — step-function costs at milestones
Healthy ratio — US vs. PK

At US salary base, people = 55–65% of spend. At PK base, people drops to ~25–30%, and infra + LLM rises to ~40–50%. Cost optimization focus shifts from headcount to routing, caching, and provider mix.

Part 6 — Downside sensitivities

Pricing pressure and power-user tax are the killers.

If $18 → $12 (Apple/Google ship $5/mo)

  • Net revenue/user: $13.50 → $9.00
  • Gross profit: $8.61 → $4.11
  • Break-even (PK): 39K → 83K users — still manageable

If churn rises to 6% steady-state

  • LTV: $207 → $143
  • LTV:CAC: 9.4× → 6.5× (still healthy)
  • Lifetime revenue −30% → Series B valuation pressure

Upside — LLM costs −50% by EOY 2026 (plausible): LLM/user $3.39 → $1.70 · gross profit +$1.69 · margin +10pp. This is likely and would help disproportionately given PK's high infra:people ratio.

Aldrich · Cost Feasibility
Part 6 — Risks specific to PK cost base

What could raise our cost advantage?

  • PKR depreciation reduces our cost base further in USD terms — upside for us.
  • PKR appreciation / wage inflation — over 3–5 years, PK tech wages rising 15–25%/yr in PKR terms could compress the advantage to ~70% from 85%. Still significant.
  • Global remote hiring pressure — if US/EU startups begin hiring senior PK engineers at USD market rates, our talent budget must rise. Happening slowly today; likely faster by 2027.
  • International hires — if we need a senior ML hire the local market can't supply, we pay USD market rates = $150K–$250K. One of these resets the ratio dramatically.
Mitigation

Budget the upper (+30%) band for Senior ML and Security roles. Allow for one remote international advisor on retainer (~$30K/yr). Lock annual comp reviews to USD, not PKR, for retention.

Part 9 — Verdict

Financially feasible — even more so at PK base.

  • Seed ≥$1M. Funds 18+ months runway to Series A milestones at PK burn.
  • Blended gross margin >45% after App Store fees. Achievable with web checkout + LLM cost control.
  • Steady-state churn <6%/month. Achievable if product delivers daily value.
  • App Store fee mitigation via web-sub flow for ≥40% of users.
  • Aggressive LLM routing from day one — default cheapest capable model, escalate only when needed.
